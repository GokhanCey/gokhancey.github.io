Selected Projects

Somnia Arcade

Somnia Arcade is a fast, fully on-chain mini-game platform built for the Somnia Testnet. Every move from revealing tiles to cashing out is recorded on-chain. Fairness is enforced with a commit–reveal randomness model using future block data. (Testnet!!)

Website Hackathon Page Demo

SeiKit App

SeiKit is a voice-driven ambient mixer that saves your sound “scenes” on the Sei EVM testnet. You speak; it switches GIF scenes, stacks up to six loops, and writes or loads your mix (scene + layers + volumes) directly on chain. Each wallet has three save slots.

Website Hackathon Page Demo

Codexa

Codexa was built to make AI answers reliable, using real data instead of guesses. Codexa connects Elastic Search with Google Gemini. You upload PDFs or text files, Codexa indexes them in Elastic, finds context, and Gemini summarizes it and giving you a verified, evidence-based answer. (Currently under development!!)
